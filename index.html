Project Title: Give your project a working title that captures its essence

This project brief initiates a design inquiry into a guiding question or goal through your proposed form of design intervention within the context of your chosen geographical location and community.

This inquiry responds to SDG. It frames the problem of the general issue you are addressing not merely as the conventional or surface-level understanding of the problem, but as your critical reframing of the problem identifying systemic or cultural roots.

The core of this investigation will be guided by a your unique theoretical lens, ethical stance or core value.

The communities central to this inquiry include the specific groups of people or communities most directly affected. Personas may be used to represent and empathise with their diverse experiences.

The potential clients and broader network of stakeholders include the wider network of individuals, organisations and non-human entities that have a stake in the problem.

The project brief also outlines design approaches through the specific design methods and practices you plan to use.
